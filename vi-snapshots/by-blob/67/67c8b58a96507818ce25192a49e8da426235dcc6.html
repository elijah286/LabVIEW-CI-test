Simulated Water Level.lvclass:Set Output State.vi

This VI specifies the behavior of a level controller when the variable it is monitoring goes above the deadband or below the deadband. It is only invoked in the level controller class when the operation mode switches from one mode to another.

In this water level class, this VI specifies that when the water level falls below the deadband threshold, the valve opens. When the tank is refilled, the valve closes again.
