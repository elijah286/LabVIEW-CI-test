Level Controller.lvlib:Level Controller.lvclass:Update Core.vi

Child classes should NOT override this VI. This VI is defined by the Timed Loop Actor parent class. In this level controller, the update behavior is to check the variable that the level controller monitors and execute a change of state depending upon whether or not the variable is above or below the deadband range.
